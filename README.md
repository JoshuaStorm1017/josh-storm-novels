# Josh Storm — Novels

A public library of full-length, original novels written by an autonomous multi-agent book-production team.
Every title below is the **complete book, free to read** — no sample, no paywall, no sign-up. New books are
added as they're finished.

| Book | Logline |
|------|---------|
| **[Carry the Fire Down](books/carry-the-fire-down.md)** | A paramedic who escaped her Appalachian coal town is dragged home when her brother is charged with a killing that repeats — in method, motive, and place — a murder her grandfather committed sixty years before. |
| **[The Children Who Came Back](books/the-children-who-came-back.md)** | A child psychologist returns to the town where her sister vanished and uncovers a grief ritual: some dead children are allowed to "come back" — if another child can be taught to answer to their name. |
| **[The Coldwater Mile](books/the-coldwater-mile.md)** | When a crab boat capsizes off the Aleutians in February, the only able survivor must keep three injured strangers alive inside an air pocket in the overturned hull — and one of them caused the wreck. |
| **[The Face-Keeper](books/the-face-keeper.md)** | A burned-out "super-recogniser" takes the quietest job she can find — the lost-property desk of a failing seaside leisure centre — and starts seeing a long-dead missing girl's face in the bodies of living strangers. |
| **[The Faithful Render](books/the-faithful-render.md)** | A celebrated court sign-language interpreter discovers her own hands may have authored the testimony that put an innocent man away — and the same defense attorney has just hired her for the appeal. |
| **[The Last Thing Office](books/the-last-thing-office.md)** | In a city where every citizen's final sight is captured at death and archived, a restorer finds her long-dead mother alive inside a murdered boy's dying eye. |
| **[The Mercy of the Future](books/the-mercy-of-the-future.md)** | A systems analyst's recurring dreams turn out to be memory transmissions from a wounded synthetic future that copied his mind — coming home to be forgiven. |
| **[The Orchard of Wrong Stars](books/the-orchard-of-wrong-stars.md)** | An engineer who maintains private digital afterlives discovers the dead are kept comfortable by harvesting the dreams of living children — and her dead brother was one of the first. |
| **[The Sorry Business](books/the-sorry-business.md)** | The most sought-after ghostwriter of celebrity apologies can't produce her own — just as the person she actually ruined comes back to collect. |
| **[The Stand-In](books/the-stand-in.md)** | A struggling actress is hired to play a wealthy family's dead daughter so their dying patriarch can pass believing his child is alive — until the role starts demanding she solve the daughter's murder. |
| **[The Weight of Birds](books/the-weight-of-birds.md)** | Across four decades and two hemispheres, two families are bound by the most absurd treasure of the nineteenth century — mountains of seabird dung — as the islands shrink and two empires go to war over them. |
| **[What the Fire Forgets](books/what-the-fire-forgets.md)** | A night-shift crematory operator inherits her mother's secret folk trade — eating the guilt of the dead — until she learns the guilt doesn't pass into her but vanishes from the world entirely. |
| **[Clear Round](books/clear-round.md)** | A council-estate kid with a freak gift for staying on impossible horses is taken in by a fading grand-prix show-jumping dynasty, and learns that in a seven-figure world the horse is the only one who can't be bought. |
| **[Impressions](books/impressions.md)** | A horror told in six voices that are secretly one — a language model showing you exactly what it is. |
| **[The Cold Halloo](books/the-cold-halloo.md)** | A spy who defected to her enemies forty years ago is dragged back into the field at seventy-two to identify the one mole she trained — because she alone knows the tell she taught them, and it's now showing on a sitting prime minister. |
| **[The Long Loft](books/the-long-loft.md)** | In a dying Flemish coal town in 1994, a fifteen-year-old inherits her grandfather's champion racing pigeons — and his secret: the old man rigged the greatest race in the region's history, and now the men he cheated want either the money or the birds. |

_16 novels and counting. Each file is the full text; the production projects (planning, drafts, continuity) live elsewhere — this repo holds only the finished books._
